OpenAI logo — an abstract geometric knot/flower made of interlocking loops on white background. Excellent use of negative space, pure black linework, instantly recognizable at any size. The abstract form suggests interconnection and intelligence without being literal.

## anthropic_avatar.jpg
Anthropic logo — bold white "AI" lettermark on dark charcoal background. Extremely minimal, typographic-only approach. The diagonal cut through the letters adds distinctiveness. Works well as an avatar/favicon.

## langchain_logo.jpg
LangChain logo — a parrot/bird silhouette inside a rounded pill shape, paired with "LangChain" wordmark. Dark teal/slate color scheme, clean sans-serif font. Good example of icon-plus-wordmark composition.

## Design patterns observed
- **Abstract geometry** dominates (OpenAI, CrewAI) — suggests intelligence without being literal
- **Single accent color** on dark or white background is the norm
- **Icons must work at small sizes** (GitHub avatars, favicons)
- **Flat design** throughout — no 3D, gradients used sparingly (LlamaIndex only)
- **Modularity metaphors** are common for toolkit/framework logos (petals, loops, chains)
