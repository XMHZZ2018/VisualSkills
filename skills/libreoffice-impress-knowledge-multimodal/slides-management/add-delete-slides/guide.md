# Add and Delete Slides

1. Open your presentation in LibreOffice Impress. The Slides panel on the left shows all current slides.
2. To insert a new slide, right-click in the Slides panel and select 'New Slide', or go to the menu Slide > Insert Slide.

   ![Step 2](step02.png)

3. To duplicate an existing slide, right-click the slide thumbnail in the Slides panel and select 'Duplicate Slide'. The copy appears directly after the original.

   ![Step 3](step03.png)

4. To reorder slides, click and drag a slide thumbnail in the Slides panel to the desired position.
5. To delete a slide, click its thumbnail in the Slides panel to select it, then press the Delete key, or right-click and select 'Delete Slide'.

   ![Step 5](step05.png)

6. To delete multiple slides at once, hold Ctrl and click each slide thumbnail to select them, then press Delete.
