# Add Slide Transition (LibreOffice Impress 7.3.7)

Slide transitions are effects and/or sounds that play between slides during a slide show, adding a professional look and smoothing the change between slides.

## Adding Transitions

1. Click **Slide Transition** in the Sidebar to open the Slide Transition deck. Read the screenshot `fig03.png` in this directory — it shows the Slide Transition deck on the Sidebar.
2. Open the presentation file to add slide transitions.
3. In the Slides pane or Slide Sorter view, select the slide to apply a transition.
4. Click the required transition from the displayed list to apply it to the selected slide.
5. In **Modify Transition**, set the following options:
   - **Variant** – select from the drop-down list (options change based on the selected transition).
   - **Duration** – enter a time for how long the transition lasts.
   - **Sound** – select a sound from the drop-down list. If selected, **Loop until next sound** becomes active; enable it to repeat the sound until another sound starts.
6. In **Advance Slide**, select one option:
   - **On mouse click** – presentation advances on each mouse click.
   - **After** – enter a time to elapse before the next slide advances automatically.
7. To apply the same transition to all slides, click **Apply Transition to All Slides**.
8. To preview the transition, click **Play**, or enable **Automatic Preview** to preview automatically when a transition is applied.

> **Tip:** A single transition type can apply to all slides, or different transitions to individual slides. Using many different transitions may look unprofessional.

> **Note:** Slides with transitions are indicated by a star in the bottom-left corner when viewed in the Slides pane or Slide Sorter view.

## Removing Transitions

1. Click **Slide Transition** in the Sidebar to open the Slide Transition deck.
2. In the Slides pane or Slide Sorter view, select the slide(s) to remove the transition from.
3. Select **None** at the top of the available transitions list.

## Playing Music During Transitions

1. Click **Slide Transition** in the Sidebar to open the Slide Transition deck.
2. Select the slide where music will be added.
3. In **Modify Transition**, select **Other sound** from the **Sound** drop-down list.
4. In the file browser, navigate to and select the required music file.
5. Click **Play** to check the file, then click **Open** to add it and close the browser.
6. To loop the music, enable **Loop until next sound** in Modify Transition.

> **Note:** Do not select **Apply Transition to All Slides** when using music, to prevent the file restarting on each slide. The music file is linked, not embedded — it must be available on any computer used to display the show.
