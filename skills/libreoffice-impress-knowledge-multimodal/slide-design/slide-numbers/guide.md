# Slide Number Formatting (LibreOffice Impress 7.3.7)

## Slide Number in the Footer

By default, the footer's right section contains the slide number field (`<number>`), labelled **Slide Number Area**.

To enable it via the Header and Footer dialog:
1. Go to **View > Master Slide** on the Menu bar.
2. Select the master slide in the Workspace.
3. Go to **Insert > Header and Footer** on the Menu bar.
4. Select **Slide number** to place the slide number in the right footer section.
5. Click **Apply** to save and close the dialog.

## Changing the Appearance (e.g., Color) of Slide Numbers

To format the text of a slide number field (including color, font, size):
1. Go to **View > Master Slide** on the Menu bar.
2. Select the master slide in the Workspace.
3. Click the slide number field to select it. The Text Formatting toolbar opens.
4. Select the field text, then apply formatting using one of:
   - The tools on the **Text Formatting toolbar**.
   - **Format** on the Menu bar and select a formatting option.
   - The **Paragraph** or **Character** dialogs.
   - The **Paragraph** and **Character** panels in the Properties deck on the Sidebar.
5. Click outside the text box when done.
6. Go to **View > Normal** or click **Close Master View** on the Master View toolbar.

Read the screenshot `fig06.png` in this directory — it shows the text formatting options available via Format on the Menu bar.

## Changing the Number Format (1, 2, 3 or a, b, c or i, ii, iii, etc.)

To change the slide number format style:
1. Go to **Slide > Slide Properties** on the Menu bar to open the Slide Properties dialog.
2. In **Layout Settings**, select the type of number format from the **Slide numbers** drop-down list.
3. Click **OK**.

Read the screenshot `fig08.png` in this directory — it shows Figure 239: Slide Properties dialog - Slide page.

## Inserting a Slide Number Field Manually

To insert a slide number as a manual field on a master slide:
1. Go to **View > Master Slide** on the Menu bar.
2. Select the master slide in the Workspace.
3. Click anywhere on the master slide without selecting an object.
4. Go to **Insert > Field** on the Menu bar and select **Slide number** from the context menu.
5. Reposition the field text box as needed.
6. Format the text as described above.
7. Click **Close Master View** or go to **View > Normal** when done.
