# Run a Slideshow

1. Open your presentation in LibreOffice Impress, then go to Slide Show > Start from First Slide (or press F5) to begin the slideshow in full-screen mode.
2. Once in full-screen mode, click anywhere or press the Right Arrow / Space Bar to advance to the next slide.
3. Press the Left Arrow or Backspace key to go back to the previous slide.
4. To jump to a specific slide, type its slide number and press Enter while in presentation mode.
5. Right-click anywhere during the slideshow to access the context menu, which lets you navigate to any slide, blank the screen, or access other presentation controls.

   Read the screenshot `step05.png` in this directory — it shows step 5.

6. Press Escape or click the X button to exit the slideshow and return to the normal editing view.
7. To configure automatic slide advancing, go to Slide > Slide Transition, then in the Advance Slide section enable 'After' and set the duration in seconds, then click 'Apply to All Slides'.

   Read the screenshot `step07.png` in this directory — it shows step 7.
