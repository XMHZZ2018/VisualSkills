# Restore Hidden UI Panels (LibreOffice Impress 7.3.7)

## Slides Pane

To display or close the Slides pane, use one of the following methods:
- Go to **View > Slides pane** on the Menu bar to toggle it on or off.
- Click the **X** in the top-right corner of the Slides pane to close it.
- Click the **Hide/Show marker** on the left side of the Workspace to hide or show the pane without closing it. Click the marker again to restore it.

## Sidebar

To display or hide the Sidebar, use one of the following methods:
- Go to **View > Sidebar** on the Menu bar.
- Use the keyboard shortcut **Ctrl+F5** (macOS: **⌘+F5**).
- Click the **Hide/Show marker** on the right side of the Workspace.
- Click the **X** in the top-right corner of the Sidebar to close it.
- Click any Sidebar icon to reopen a deck and display the Sidebar.

Read the screenshot `fig03.png` in this directory — it shows the Sidebar section of the Impress main window with the Hide/Show marker and Sidebar icons visible.

## Toolbars

To display or hide toolbars, go to **View > Toolbars** on the Menu bar and select the desired toolbar from the submenu.

## Status Bar

To show the Status Bar, go to **View** on the Menu bar and select **Status Bar**.

## View Tabs Bar

If the Normal/Outline/Notes/Slide Sorter tabs are not shown at the top of the Workspace, go to **View > Views Tab Bar** on the Menu bar.
