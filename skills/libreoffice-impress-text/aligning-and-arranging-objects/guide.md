# Aligning and arranging objects (LibreOffice Impress 7.3)

## Aligning objects

Click the triangle next to **Align Objects** on the Drawing toolbar to open the alignment sub-toolbar. Select two or more objects first.

**Horizontal alignment:**
- **Left** — aligns objects to the leftmost selected object.
- **Centered** — centers objects horizontally.
- **Right** — aligns objects to the rightmost selected object.

**Vertical alignment:**
- **Top** — aligns objects to the topmost selected object.
- **Center** — centers objects vertically.
- **Bottom** — aligns objects to the bottommost selected object.

---

## Distributing objects

**Distribute Selection** changes the spacing between objects evenly. Select a minimum of three objects, then click the triangle next to **Distribute Selection** on the Drawing toolbar.

---

## Arranging objects (stack order)

Impress organizes objects in a stack — objects on top cover those below. Change the stack level using:

- Click the arrange tool on the Line and Filling toolbar.
- Right-click > **Arrange** > select option.
- **Format > Arrange** on the Menu bar.

**Stack order options:**
- **Bring to Front** (*Ctrl+Shift++*) — moves selected object in front of all others.
- **Bring Forward** (*Ctrl++*) — moves selected object one level up.
- **Send Backward** (*Ctrl+–*) — moves selected object one level down.
- **Send to Back** (*Ctrl+Shift+–*) — moves selected object behind all others.
- **In Front of Object** — select first object, then click second; first moves in front of second.
- **Behind Object** — select first object, then click second; first moves behind second.
- **Reverse** — swaps the stacking order of two selected objects.

## Snap to grid

Use **Snap to Grid** to move objects exactly onto grid points.

**Toggle Snap to Grid:**
- **View > Snap Guides > Snap to Grid**
- Right-click on slide > **Snap Guides > Snap to Grid**
- Click **Snap to Grid** on the Options toolbar.

**Display grid:**
- **View > Grid and Helplines > Display Grid**
- Click **Display Grid** on the Options toolbar.

**Configure snap range:**
1. Go to **Tools > Options > LibreOffice Impress > Grid**.
2. Set the *Snap range* (default: 5 pixels).
3. Click **OK**.

---

## Snap points and lines

Snap lines and snap points position objects at specific slide locations. They appear as dashed lines/crosses and don't print.

**Inserting snap points/lines:**
- **Insert > Snap Guide**
- Right-click empty space > **Insert Snap Guide**

**Options in New Snap Object dialog:**
- **Position** — X and Y relative to top-left corner.
- **Type** — Point, Vertical (line), or Horizontal (line).

**Display snap guides:**
- **View > Snap Guides > Display Snap Guides**
- Click **Display Snap Guides** on the Options toolbar.

**Editing:** Right-click snap point > **Edit Snap Point** — enter new coordinates or drag to new position.

**Deleting:** Right-click > **Delete Snap Point** or **Delete Snap Line**.

**Tip:** Display rulers with **View > Rulers**. Click and drag from a ruler onto the slide to quickly insert a snap line.

---

## Using helplines

Helplines extend from object edges to the rulers when moving an object. They help position objects but have no snap function.

**Enable helplines:**
- **Tools > Options > LibreOffice Impress > View** — select *Helplines while moving*.
- Click **Helplines While Moving** on the Options toolbar.
- Right-click > **Grid and Helplines > Helplines While Moving**.
