# Slide Transitions & Object Animations (LibreOffice Impress 7.3.7)

## Slide Transitions

To add a transition, click **Slide Transition** on the Sidebar to open the Slide Transition deck. Select a slide in the **Slides** pane or **Slide Sorter** view, then pick a transition style from the displayed list. Under **Modify Transition**, you can choose a **Variant**, set the **Duration**, and optionally attach a **Sound**. If you select a sound, the **Loop until next sound** option becomes available.

In the **Advance Slide** section, choose between **On mouse click** (manual advance) or **After** with a time value for automatic advance. To apply the same transition everywhere, hit **Apply Transition to All Slides**. Use **Play** or check **Automatic Preview** to see how it looks. Slides with transitions show a small star icon in the **Slides** pane.

Visual reference: the Slide Transition deck on the Sidebar showing transition styles, Modify Transition settings for Variant, Duration, and Sound, and the Advance Slide options.

To remove a transition, open the Slide Transition deck, select the slide, and choose **None** at the top of the transitions list.

You can also use transitions to play background music: select a slide, choose **Other sound** from the **Sound** drop-down, browse to your audio file, and enable **Loop until next sound** for continuous playback.

## Object Animations

Animations target individual elements on a slide — text boxes, shapes, images, charts. Switch to **View > Normal**, select the object, then open the **Animation** deck on the Sidebar (or right-click and choose **Animation**). Click **Add Effect** (the **+** button) to register the object in the animation list.

Pick a **Category** (Entrance, Emphasis, Exit, Motion Paths), then choose a specific **Effect**. Set **Start** to *On click*, *With previous*, or *After previous* to control sequencing. Adjust **Direction**, **Duration**, and **Delay** to taste. Reorder effects using the **Move Up** / **Move Down** arrows. Hit **Play** to preview, or enable **Automatic Preview** for live feedback as you tweak settings.

Visual reference: the Animation deck on the Sidebar with multiple animated objects listed, Category and Effect dropdowns, and Start, Direction, Duration, and Delay controls.

For finer control, click **Options** (next to the **Direction** drop-down) to open the **Effect Options** dialog. The **Effect** tab lets you set a **Sound**, choose what happens **After animation** (dim with color, hide, etc.), and control **Text animation** mode (all at once, word by word, or letter by letter). The **Timing** tab exposes **Repeat** options (*none*, *Until next click*, *Until end of slide*) and a **Rewind when done playing** toggle. The **Text Animation** tab controls **Group text** granularity — animate as one object, all paragraphs at once, or by 1st level paragraphs — with an optional delay between groups.

Visual reference: the Effect Options dialog on the Effect tab showing Sound, After animation, and Text animation settings.

To change an animation, select the element in the **Animation** box and adjust its properties. To remove one, select it and click **Remove Effect**. Keep in mind: animations cannot be applied to elements on master slides — you'll need to apply them per-slide or copy animated elements between slides.
