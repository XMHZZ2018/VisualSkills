# View and Clear Browsing History

1. Open Chrome and press `Ctrl+H` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+Y` (Mac) to open History, or navigate to `chrome://history`
2. Browse the full list of visited pages, sorted by date and time
3. Use the search bar at the top to search history by keyword or site name
4. To delete individual entries, hover over an item, check the checkbox, then click **Delete** in the top-right
5. To clear all browsing data, click the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top-right → **More tools** → **Clear browsing data**, or press `Ctrl+Shift+Delete` (Windows/Linux) / `Cmd+Shift+Delete` (Mac), or navigate to `chrome://settings/clearBrowserData`
6. In the dialog, set the **Time range** (Last hour / Last 24 hours / Last 7 days / Last 4 weeks / All time)
7. Check **Browsing history** and any other data types to remove (cookies, cached files)
8. Click **Clear data** to confirm
