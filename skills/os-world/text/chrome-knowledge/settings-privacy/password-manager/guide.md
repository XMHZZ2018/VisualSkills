# Use Chrome Password Manager

1. Navigate to `chrome://password-manager/passwords` to open Google Password Manager.
2. Click the **Settings** gear icon in the left sidebar to configure password saving preferences.
3. Toggle **Offer to save passwords** on to enable automatic save prompts when you log in to sites.
4. To view a saved password, click on any credential in the list, then click the **eye icon** to reveal the password.
5. To edit a saved password, open the credential, click **Edit**, update the username or password, then click **Save**.
6. To delete a saved password, open the credential and click **Delete**, or click the three-dot menu next to it and select **Delete**.
7. To manually add a password, click **Add** (top right), enter the website, username, and password, then click **Save**.
8. To check for compromised passwords, go to `chrome://password-manager/checkup` and review flagged credentials.
9. To export passwords, go to `chrome://password-manager/settings`, click **Export passwords**, and confirm your identity when prompted.
10. To import passwords, click **Import passwords** on the same settings page and select a `.csv` file.
