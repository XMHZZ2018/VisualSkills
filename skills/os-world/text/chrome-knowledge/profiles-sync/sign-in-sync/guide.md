# Sign In and Enable Sync

1. Click the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top-right corner of Chrome.
2. Click **Settings**.
3. In the left sidebar, click **Privacy and security**.
4. Scroll down and click **Site settings**.
5. Scroll to the bottom and click **Additional content settings**.
6. Scroll down and click **On-device site data** (or search "site data" in Settings).
7. Select **Allow sites to save data on your device**.
8. Under the "Allow to save data on your device" section, click **Add**.
9. Type `accounts.google.com` and click **Add**.
10. Close and reopen Chrome — sync should resume without requiring sign-in.

**If the issue persists:**

11. Go to **Settings > Privacy and security > Clear browsing data**.
12. Set the time range to **All time**, select all options, and click **Clear data**.
13. Close and reopen Chrome to verify you remain signed in and sync is active.
