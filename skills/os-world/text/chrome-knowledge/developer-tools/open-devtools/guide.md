# Open DevTools

1. Press `F12` to open DevTools instantly.
2. Or press `Ctrl+Shift+I` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+Option+I` (Mac) to open DevTools.
3. Or press `Ctrl+Shift+J` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+Option+J` (Mac) to open DevTools directly to the Console tab.
4. Or right-click anywhere on a webpage and select **Inspect**.
5. Or click the three-dot menu → **More tools** → **Developer tools**.
