# Fixing Missing Windows and Dialogs (GIMP 2.10)

If all your tool docks — Toolbox, Layers, Tool Options — have vanished and you're staring at nothing but a bare image window, you probably hit **Tab** by accident. That key toggles all docks on and off. Just press **Tab** again to bring everything back. You can also use **Windows → Hide Docks** to toggle visibility from the menu. Note: if **Tab** doesn't respond, click the image canvas first to make sure it has focus, then try again.

If only the **Tool Options** dialog is gone, click the small triangle at the top-right corner of any dock to open its menu, choose **Add Tab**, then select **Tool Options**. Alternatively, go to **Windows → Dockable Dialogs → Tool Options** — though this may place it in a different dock. If it lands in the wrong spot, just drag the **Tool Options** tab to the dock where you want it. To prevent future accidents, open that same triangle menu and enable **Lock Tab to Dock**.

When some tool icons seem to be missing from the Toolbox, they're likely just grouped. Look for a small triangle in the bottom-right corner of a tool icon — hover or click it to reveal the other tools in that group. If you'd rather see every icon individually, go to **Edit → Preferences → Toolbox** and disable grouping there.

In single-window mode, if the tab bar showing your open images disappears from the top of the canvas, head to **Windows → Show Tabs** and make sure it's checked.
