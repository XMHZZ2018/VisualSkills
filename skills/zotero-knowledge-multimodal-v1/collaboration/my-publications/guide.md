# Publishing with My Publications (Zotero 7)

My Publications lets you build a public bibliography on your zotero.org profile page, showcasing your own research. Think of it as a simple academic portfolio — visitors can browse your work and, if you allow it, download attached files directly.

To get started, find the **My Publications** collection in the left pane under **My Library** (it sits alongside Duplicate Items, Unfiled Items, and Trash). Drag any item you want to share into that collection. This kicks off the My Publications helper wizard.

See `fig01.png`.

The helper first asks whether to **Include files** and **Include notes** alongside the item's metadata. Links — such as a DOI link or a URL to your personal site — are always shared automatically. If an item has no attachment or link, Zotero will generate a link from the item's DOI or URL field instead. Check the box confirming you created the work and have distribution rights, then click **Next: Sharing**.

If you opted to include files, the next screen asks how your work may be shared. You have three choices: **No, only publish my work on zotero.org** (all rights reserved), **Yes, under a Creative Commons license**, or **Yes, and place my work in the public domain**. The all-rights-reserved option simply hosts the file on zotero.org without granting others redistribution permission. Public domain (CC0) is irreversible, so read the CC0 FAQ before choosing it.

See `fig02.png`.

If you pick Creative Commons, one more screen appears. You'll answer two questions: whether to allow adaptations of your work (no, yes with share-alike, or yes) and whether to allow commercial use. Zotero assembles the matching CC license automatically and displays it. Keep in mind that a CC license, once applied, cannot be revoked — even if you later change terms or remove the item.

Once you've made your choices, click **Add to My Publications**. The item (and any included files) will sync to your public profile on the next Zotero sync. You can adjust what's shown at any time by revisiting the My Publications collection and removing or re-adding items. For sharing items you didn't author, use **Zotero Groups** instead.
