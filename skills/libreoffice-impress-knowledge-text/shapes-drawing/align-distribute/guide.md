# Align and Distribute Objects

1. Select the objects you want to align by holding **Shift** and clicking each one, or drag to select all.
2. Navigate to **Format > Position and Size** or use the **Align and Distribute** toolbar.
3. To open the Align and Distribute dialog, go to **Format > Align and Distribute**.
4. Choose an alignment option:
   - **Left**, **Center**, or **Right** to align horizontally
   - **Top**, **Middle**, or **Bottom** to align vertically
5. To align relative to the slide, select **Align to Page** in the dialog before applying.
6. To align relative to each other, ensure **Align to Page** is unchecked.
7. To distribute objects evenly, click **Distribute Horizontally** or **Distribute Vertically** in the same dialog.
8. Horizontal distribution equalizes spacing between left/right edges; vertical distribution equalizes top/bottom spacing.
9. Click **Close** to apply and dismiss the dialog.
10. To align using the right-click menu, select objects, right-click, and choose **Position and Size > Align**.
