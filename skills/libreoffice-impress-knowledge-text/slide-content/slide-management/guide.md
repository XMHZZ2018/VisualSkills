# Manage Slides (Duplicate, Summary) (LibreOffice Impress 7.3.7)

## Duplicating Slides

Duplicating preserves formatting, layout, and animations from the selected slide. The duplicate is inserted immediately after the selected slide.

1. Open Normal or Slide Sorter view.
2. Select the slide(s) to duplicate in the Slides pane or Slide Sorter view.
3. Use one of:
   - **Slide > Duplicate Slide** on the Menu bar
   - Right-click the slide and select **Duplicate Slide**

**Tip:** Duplicate a crowded slide to split information across two slides — all formatting and backgrounds are preserved.

## Creating a Summary Slide

A summary slide uses slide titles as bullet points, useful for creating an agenda.

1. Select the slide to appear first in the summary.
2. Go to **Slide > Summary Slide** on the Menu bar.
3. A summary slide is created at the end of the presentation with bullet points from all slide titles.
4. Move the summary slide to the desired position if needed.

**Note:** The selected slide must contain an outline for this to work.

## Expanding a Slide

Splits a slide with too many points into multiple slides.

1. Select the slide in the Slides pane.
2. Go to **Slide > Expand Slide** on the Menu bar.
   - First outline level items become titles of new slides.
   - Lower outline levels move up one level in the new slides.
3. Rearrange the new slides as needed.

**Note:** The selected slide must contain an outline.

## Copying Slides Between Presentations

1. Open both presentations in **Slide Sorter** view.
2. Select and copy slides: **Edit > Copy**, right-click **Copy**, or **Ctrl+C** (macOS **⌘+C**).
3. In the target presentation, select the insertion point.
4. Paste: **Edit > Paste**, right-click **Paste**, or **Ctrl+V** (macOS **⌘+V**).

To drag between presentations: hold **Ctrl** (macOS **⌘**) while dragging to copy; drag without modifier to move.
