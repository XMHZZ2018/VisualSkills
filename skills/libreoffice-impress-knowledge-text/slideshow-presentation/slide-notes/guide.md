# Add Speaker Notes

1. Open your presentation in LibreOffice Impress.
2. In the Slides pane on the left, click the slide you want to add notes to.
3. Click the **Notes** text area at the bottom of the main workspace (below the slide). If it is not visible, navigate to **View > Notes**.
4. Click inside the notes area and type your speaker notes.
5. Repeat for each slide by selecting the next slide in the Slides pane.
6. To view notes during a presentation, start the slideshow via **Slide Show > Start from First Slide**, then use **Presenter Console** (enable via **Slide Show > Presenter Console**).
