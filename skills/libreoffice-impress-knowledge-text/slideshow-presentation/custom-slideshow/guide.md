# Create Custom Slideshows

1. Open your presentation in LibreOffice Impress.
2. Navigate to **Slide Show > Custom Slide Show** from the menu bar.
3. Click **New** to create a new custom show.
4. Enter a name for your custom slideshow in the **Name** field.
5. In the **Existing Slides** list, select each slide you want to include.
6. Click **Add >>** to move selected slides to the **Slides in Custom Show** list.
7. To reorder slides, select a slide in the right list and use the arrow buttons to move it up or down.
8. To remove a slide, select it in the right list and click **Remove**.
9. Click **OK** to save the custom show.
10. To run it, go to **Slide Show > Custom Slide Show**, select your show from the list, and click **Start**.
