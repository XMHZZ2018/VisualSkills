# Restore Hidden UI Panels (LibreOffice Impress 7.3.7)

## Slides Pane

- **View > Slides pane** on the Menu bar to show or hide
- Click the **X** in the upper right corner of the pane to close
- Click the **Hide/Show marker** on the left of the Workspace to hide/show (does not close the pane; click again to restore)

## Sidebar

- **View > Sidebar** on the Menu bar
- Keyboard shortcut: **Ctrl+F5** (macOS: **⌘+F5**)
- Click the **Hide/Show marker** on the right of the Workspace (click again to restore)
- Click the **X** in the upper right corner to close
- To reopen after closing: click any Sidebar icon to open a deck

## Toolbars

- **View > Toolbars** on the Menu bar, then select the toolbar from the submenu
- To show/hide individual toolbar buttons: right-click an empty area on the toolbar and select **Visible Buttons**

## View Tabs Bar (Normal / Outline / Notes / Slide Sorter)

- If workspace view tabs are not visible: **View > Views Tab Bar** on the Menu bar

## Status Bar

- To restore if hidden: **View** on the Menu bar, then select **Status Bar**
