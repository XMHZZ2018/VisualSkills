# Add Header and Footer

1. Open your presentation in LibreOffice Impress.
2. Navigate to **Insert > Header and Footer** from the menu bar.
3. In the dialog, check **Date and time** to add a date field — choose **Fixed** (static date) or **Variable** (auto-updates).
4. Check **Slide number** to insert automatic slide numbering on each slide.
5. Check **Footer** and type your desired footer text in the text field.
6. Click **Apply to All** to add to every slide, or **Apply** for the current slide only.
7. To exclude the title slide, check **Do not show on first slide**.
8. To edit footer text boxes directly, click on a footer/header text box on any slide and type your changes.
9. To insert a slide number field manually, click a text box, then go to **Insert > Header and Footer** or **Insert > Field > Page Number**.
10. To insert a date field manually, place your cursor in a text box and select **Insert > Field > Date (Fixed)** or **Date (Variable)**.
