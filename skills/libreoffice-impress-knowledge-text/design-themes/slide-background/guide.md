# Set Slide Background

1. Open your LibreOffice Impress presentation.
2. Click **Slide** menu > **Slide Properties** (or **Format** > **Page Style**).
3. In the dialog, go to the **Area** tab.
4. Choose a background type:
   - **Color** — select a solid color from the palette.
   - **Gradient** — pick a preset or customize start/end colors.
   - **Bitmap** — use a built-in image or click **Add/Import** to browse for your own image file, then click **Open**.
   - **Pattern** or **Hatch** — select from the available presets.
5. For bitmap/image backgrounds, adjust size under the **Size** section (width/height) and set position via the **Position** drop-down.
6. Preview the result in the right-side preview pane.
7. Click **Apply** to apply to the current slide, or **OK** to confirm and close.
