# Apply Master Slide

1. Open your presentation in LibreOffice Impress.
2. Navigate to **View > Master > Slide Master** to enter Master View, or use the Slide Panel on the right.
3. To apply a built-in master slide, open the **Master Slides** panel: go to **View > Sidebar** and select the **Master Pages** section.
4. In the Master Pages panel, browse the available master slides under "Available for Use."
5. Click on any master slide thumbnail to apply it to all slides in the presentation.
6. To apply a master slide to only selected slides, right-click the desired master thumbnail and choose **Apply to Selected Slides**.
7. To use a custom or downloaded template, go to **Slide > Insert Slide from File** or **File > Templates > Manage Templates** and import the `.otp` file.
8. To access masters via menu: go to **Slide > Slide Properties > Background** or **Slide > Set Background Image** for visual customization tied to the master.
9. Exit Master View by clicking **Close Master View** in the toolbar or going to **View > Normal**.
