# Text Alignment (LibreOffice Impress 7.3.7)

> **Note:** The provided source pages cover general text formatting, the Paragraph dialog, and the Sidebar Paragraph panel, but do not contain specific procedures for left/center/right text alignment. The following reflects only what the source states.

## Select Text First

- Click the **text box border** to select all text in the box, or
- Click inside and drag, double-click (word), or triple-click (paragraph) to select partial text

## Apply Paragraph Formatting (includes alignment)

**Option 1 — Text Formatting toolbar**
Use the alignment tools directly on the **Text Formatting toolbar**. Changes are immediate.

**Option 2 — Paragraph dialog**
1. Select the text or paragraph
2. Open via **Format > Paragraph** on the Menu bar, or right-click selected text and choose **Paragraph**
3. Use the formatting options on the tabbed pages
4. Click **OK** to apply and close
5. Click outside the text box to deselect

**Option 3 — Sidebar Paragraph panel**
1. Click the text box border to show selection handles
2. On the Sidebar, click **Properties** to open the Properties deck
3. Click **Paragraph** to open the Paragraph panel
4. Apply formatting — changes are immediate
5. Click **More Options** to open the full Paragraph dialog if needed
