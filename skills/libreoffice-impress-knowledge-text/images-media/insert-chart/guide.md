# Insert Charts and Graphs

1. Open your LibreOffice Impress presentation and navigate to the slide where you want to insert a chart.
2. Click **Insert > Chart** from the menu bar.
3. Double-click the chart placeholder that appears to enter edit mode (a gray border indicates you are inside the chart editor).
4. In the **Chart Wizard** dialog, select a chart type (Bar, Pie, Line, Column, etc.) from the list on the left.
5. Choose a chart subtype (e.g., stacked bar, 3D pie) from the options on the right.
6. Click **Next** to configure the data range, or select **Data Table** to enter your data directly.
7. Enter or paste your data values into the data table rows and columns.
8. Click **Next** to set data series options (assign rows or columns as data series).
9. Click **Next** again to add chart titles, axis labels, and toggle the legend.
10. Click **Finish** to insert the chart.
11. Click outside the chart to deselect and return to the slide editor.
12. To edit the chart later, double-click it to re-enter edit mode.
13. To resize the chart, single-click it and drag the corner handles.
14. To change the chart type after insertion, double-click the chart, then go to **Insert > Chart Type**.
15. To modify data, double-click the chart, then go to **View > Chart Data Table**.
