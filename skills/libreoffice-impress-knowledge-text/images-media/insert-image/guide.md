# Insert and Resize Images

1. Insert an image via **Insert > Image > From File**, navigate to your file, and click **Open**.
2. To insert from clipboard, use **Ctrl+V** to paste directly onto the slide.
3. Click the image once to select it.
4. To resize precisely, double-click the image to open **Position and Size** (or press **F4**).
5. Under **Size**, check **Keep Ratio** to maintain aspect ratio, then edit the **Width** field — Height adjusts automatically.
6. To set Width and Height independently, uncheck **Keep Ratio**, then enter values for both fields.
7. Click **OK** to apply.
8. To resize freehand, click the image once, then drag a corner handle — dragging a corner preserves aspect ratio.
9. Dragging a side, top, or bottom handle stretches the image and does **not** preserve aspect ratio.
10. To crop, right-click the image and select **Crop Image**, then drag the crop handles to trim edges.
