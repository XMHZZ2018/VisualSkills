# Export to PDF

1. Open your presentation in LibreOffice Impress.
2. Click **File** in the menu bar.
3. Select **Export as PDF...** from the dropdown menu.
4. In the PDF Options dialog, configure quality settings as needed (compression, image quality, etc.).
5. Click **Export**.
6. Choose a save location and filename in the file dialog.
7. Click **Save**.

**Alternative:** Click the **Export as PDF** button directly on the toolbar (PDF icon) to skip the options dialog and export with default settings.
