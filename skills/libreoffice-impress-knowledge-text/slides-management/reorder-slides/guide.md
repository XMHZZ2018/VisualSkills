# Reorder Slides

## Method 1: Drag-and-Drop in Slide Panel

1. Locate the Slides panel on the left side of the screen.
2. Click and hold the slide you want to move.
3. Drag it up or down to the desired position in the panel.
4. Release the mouse button to drop the slide in the new position.

## Method 2: Using the Slide Panel Context Menu

1. In the Slides panel on the left, right-click the slide you want to move.
2. Select **Move Slide** or use **Cut** to remove the slide.
3. Right-click on the slide position where you want to insert it.
4. Select **Paste** to place the slide in the new location.

## Method 3: Slide Sorter View

1. Navigate to **View > Slide Sorter**.
2. Click and drag slides to rearrange their order.
3. Release to drop each slide in the desired position.
4. Return to normal view via **View > Normal**.

## Method 4: Move Slide via Menu

1. Click the slide you want to move in the Slides panel.
2. Navigate to **Slide > Move Slide** (or right-click > **Move Slide**).
3. Enter the target slide number in the dialog.
4. Click **OK** to confirm.
