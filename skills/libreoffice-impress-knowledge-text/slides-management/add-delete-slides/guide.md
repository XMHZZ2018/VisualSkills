# Add and Delete Slides

1. Open LibreOffice Impress with your presentation.
2. **Insert a new slide:** Right-click on a slide in the Slides panel (left side) and select **New Slide**, or go to **Slide > Insert Slide**.
3. **Insert via menu:** Navigate to the menu bar and select **Slide > Insert Slide at End** to append a slide.
4. **Duplicate a slide:** Right-click the slide in the Slides panel and select **Duplicate Slide**.
5. **Move a slide:** Click and drag the slide thumbnail to a new position in the Slides panel.
6. **Delete a slide:** Right-click the slide thumbnail in the Slides panel and select **Delete Slide**.
7. **Delete multiple slides:** Hold `Ctrl` and click each slide to select multiple, then right-click and select **Delete Slide**.
8. **Undo a deletion:** Press `Ctrl+Z` to restore a deleted slide.
