# Apply Slide Layouts

1. Open LibreOffice Impress with an existing or new presentation.
2. In the slide panel on the left, click the slide you want to apply a layout to.
3. Navigate to **View > Normal** to ensure you are in the main editing view.
4. Open the Layouts panel: go to **View > Sidebar** and select the **Layouts** section, or go to **Slide > Layout** from the menu bar.
5. In the Layouts panel, browse the available predefined layouts (Title Slide, Title Content, Blank, Two Content, etc.).
6. Click the desired layout thumbnail to apply it to the selected slide.
7. To apply a layout via right-click: right-click the slide in the slide panel, select **Layout**, then choose the desired layout from the submenu.
8. To apply the same layout to multiple slides, hold **Ctrl** and click each slide in the slide panel before selecting the layout.
9. If placeholder boxes shift or disappear after applying a layout, go to **Insert > Header and Footer** or manually reposition text boxes as needed.
