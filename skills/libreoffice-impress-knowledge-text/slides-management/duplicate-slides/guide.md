# Duplicate Slides

1. Right-click the slide you want to duplicate in the Slides panel.
2. Select **Move or Copy Slide** or **Duplicate Slide** from the context menu.
3. Alternatively, select the slide and go to **Slide > Duplicate Slide** in the menu bar.
4. To duplicate multiple slides, hold **Ctrl** and click each slide in the Slides panel to select them, then right-click and choose **Duplicate Slide**.
5. The duplicated slide(s) will appear immediately after the original, preserving all content and formatting.
