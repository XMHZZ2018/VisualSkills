# Set Slide Background Color (LibreOffice Impress 7.3.7)

## Change background for a single slide

1. In Normal view, right-click an empty area on the slide and select **Slide Properties**, or go to **Slide > Slide Properties** on the Menu bar.
2. Select the **Background** tab.
3. Choose a background type: **Color**, **Gradient**, **Bitmap**, **Pattern**, or **Hatch**.
4. Configure the options, then click **OK**.

## Change background via Sidebar

1. In Normal view, click **Properties** in the Sidebar to open the Properties deck.
2. Click **Slide** to open the Slide panel.
3. Select the background type from the **Background** drop-down list.

## Change background on a master slide (affects all slides using that master)

**Using Slide Properties:**
1. Go to **View > Master Slide** on the Menu bar.
2. Select a master slide in the Slides pane.
3. Go to **Slide > Slide Properties** or right-click in the Workspace and select **Slide Properties**.
4. Select the **Background** tab.
5. Choose a background type: **Color**, **Gradient**, **Bitmap**, **Pattern**, or **Hatch**.
6. Click **OK**.

**Using Background style:**
1. Go to **View > Master Slide** on the Menu bar.
2. Select a master slide in the Slides pane.
3. Click **Styles** on the Sidebar, then click **Presentation Styles**.
4. Right-click **Background** style and select **Modify** to open the Background dialog.
5. Choose a background type: **Color**, **Gradient**, **Bitmap**, **Pattern**, or **Hatch**.
6. Click **OK**.
