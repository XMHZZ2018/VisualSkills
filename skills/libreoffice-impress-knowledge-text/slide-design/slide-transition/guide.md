# Add Slide Transition (LibreOffice Impress 7.3.7)

## Add a Transition

1. Click **Slide Transition** in the Sidebar to open the Slide Transition deck.
2. In the **Slides pane** or **Slide Sorter view**, select the slide to apply a transition.
3. Click the required transition from the displayed list.
4. In **Modify Transition**, configure:
   - **Variant** — select from the drop-down list (options vary by transition).
   - **Duration** — enter how long the transition lasts.
   - **Sound** — select a sound to play when the transition activates. Enable **Loop until next sound** to repeat the sound until another starts.
5. In **Advance Slide**, select one:
   - **On mouse click** — advances to the next slide on each mouse click.
   - **After** — enter a time for the slide to advance automatically.
6. To apply the same transition to all slides, click **Apply Transition to All Slides**.
7. Click **Play** to preview the transition, or enable **Automatic Preview** to preview automatically on selection.

## Remove a Transition

1. Click **Slide Transition** in the Sidebar.
2. In the **Slides pane** or **Slide Sorter view**, select the slide(s).
3. Select **None** at the top of the transitions list.

## Notes

- Slides with transitions show a star in the bottom-left corner in the **Slides pane** and **Slide Sorter view**.
- A single transition can be applied to all slides, or different transitions to individual slides. Using many different transitions may look unprofessional.
- A time must be entered in **After** to enable the **Loop and repeat after** option in Presentation Mode for continuous automatic playback.
