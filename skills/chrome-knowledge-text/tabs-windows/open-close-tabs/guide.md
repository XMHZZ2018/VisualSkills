# Open and Close Tabs

1. Open a new tab: Click the **+** button next to the last tab, or press `Ctrl+T` (Windows) / `Cmd+T` (Mac).
2. Close a tab: Click the **×** on the tab, or press `Ctrl+W` (Windows) / `Cmd+W` (Mac).
3. Reopen a recently closed tab: Press `Ctrl+Shift+T` (Windows) / `Cmd+Shift+T` (Mac).
4. To restore all tabs on browser restart, click the **three-dot menu** (top-right corner).
5. Select **Settings**.
6. Navigate to `chrome://settings/onStartup` or click **On startup** in the left sidebar.
7. Select **Continue where you left off**.
