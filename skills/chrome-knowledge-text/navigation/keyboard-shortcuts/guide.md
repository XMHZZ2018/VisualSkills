# Keyboard Shortcuts

1. Open a new tab: press `Ctrl+T` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+T` (Mac)
2. Close the current tab: press `Ctrl+W` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+W` (Mac)
3. Cycle to the next tab (left to right): press `Ctrl+Tab` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+Option+Right` (Mac)
4. Cycle to the previous tab (right to left): press `Ctrl+Shift+Tab` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+Option+Left` (Mac)
5. Open your Chrome home page in the current tab: press `Alt+Home` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+Shift+H` (Mac)
6. Reopen the most recently closed tab: press `Ctrl+Shift+T` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+Shift+T` (Mac)
7. Close the entire browser window: press `Ctrl+Shift+W` (Windows/Linux) or `Cmd+Shift+W` (Mac)
